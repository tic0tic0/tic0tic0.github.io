Zug is Germany's highest peak at 2,963 meters. It is located on the border between Germany and Austria and means "top of train" in German.For a long period of history, no one dared to explore the mountain because of its precipitous peak, shrouded in clouds and complicated terrain.It is said that chug is not a place to explore, and that those who want to conquer it never come back.
楚格峰是德国的最高峰,海拔高度为2963米,位于德国和奥地利的边界地带,德语有“火车顶”之意。由于山峰险峻,云雾缭绕,地形复杂,很长一段历史时期内无人敢去探山。人们都说,楚格峰是不欢迎人们去探山的,想征服它的人从来都是有去无回。
